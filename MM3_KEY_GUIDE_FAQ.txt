[SEC1] EXPLORATION & OVERWORLD
===============================================================================

* MOVE:          D-Pad (or LT + Joystick). 
* SIDESTEP:      LT + RT + Joystick. (Uses Ctrl+Arrow logic).
* INTERACT:      RT (SPACE).
* SHOOT/BASH:    LT + D-UP. (Click to Shoot / Hold to Bash).
* REST/DISMISS:  LT + D-DOWN. (Click to Rest / Hold to Dismiss).

* QUICK CHARTS:  
    - TAP Z:     Quick Reference Chart (Q).
    - HOLD Z:    General Information (I).
    - TAP Y:     View Map (M).
    - HOLD Y:    Corak's Notes (V).

* MOUSE EMULATION: 
    - POINT:     Left Joystick (Moves cursor).
    - CLICK:     Hold X (Registers click).

===============================================================================
[SEC2] COMBAT & QUICK FIGHT
===============================================================================

* STANDARD:      A (Attack), B (Quick Fight), C (Cast).
* QUICK FIGHT:   Executes a pre-set action (Attack/Block/Cast/Retreat).
    - CYCLE:     Hold B + D-RIGHT to rotate stances.
    - LOCK/SET:  Hold B + D-DOWN or press START.
* OTHER MOVES:   D-UP + A (Block) | D-DOWN + A (Retreat).

===============================================================================
[SEC3] INVENTORY & PARTY MANAGEMENT
===============================================================================
* SELECT CHAR:   LT + Button (Selects Party Position 1-8).
    - FRONT ROW: [X: 1] [Y: 2] [Z: 3] 
    - BACK ROW:  [C: 4] [B: 5] [A: 6]
    - HIRLINGS:  [START: 7] [SELECT: 8]
    
    * QUICK TAP: Character Info Screen.
    * LONG HOLD: Character Inventory Bag.

* FAST-NAV PARTY MODE (Hold C):
    - NEXT CHAR:  B (Cycles 1-8 Forward).
    - LAST CHAR:  A (Cycles 8-1 Backward).

* STANDARD BAG NAV MODE (Hold RT):
    - MOVE LIST:  D-UP / D-DOWN (Single Item).
    - PAGE UP/DN: JOY-UP / JOY-DOWN (Page Up / Page Down).
    - EQUIP/REM:  D-RIGHT (Equip) / D-LEFT (Remove).
    - USE/DROP:   D-UP (Use) / D-DOWN (Discard).
* TRANSFER: While holding RT, click the recipient's button.

* FAST-NAV BAG MODE (Hold SELECT):
    - CYCLE CHAR: Tap LT (Previous) / Tap RT (Next).
    - MOVE LIST:  Tap Y (Up) / Tap B (Down).
    - PAGE UP/DN: Tap Z (Page Up) / Tap C (Page Down).
    - EQUIP/REM:  Tap A (Click for Equip / Hold for Remove).
    - USE/DROP:   Tap X (Click for Use / Hold for Discard).
    - EXIT BAG:   Tap START.

* STAT DETAILS: On Character Info screen, use D-PAD to highlight a stat, 
                then press A for detailed attribute info.

===============================================================================
[SEC4] SPELL MENU & ELEMENTAL SPELLS
===============================================================================

* OPEN MENU:     C (Click for Current / Hold for New).
* MOVE LIST:     D-UP / D-DOWN (Single Item).
* PAGE UP/DN:    RT + JOY-UP / JOY-DOWN (Scrolls List).
* SELECT/CAST:   START (Confirm) or C again.
* SELECT ELEMENT (C-HOLD): D-UP: Fire | D-RIGHT: Elec | D-DOWN: Cold | D-LEFT: Acid.

* FAST-NAV SPELLS (Hold SELECT):
    - CYCLE CHAR: Tap LT (Prev) / Tap RT (Next).
    - MOVE LIST:  Tap Y (Up) / Tap B (Down).
    - PAGE UP/DN: Tap Z (Page Up) / Tap C (Page Down).
    - EXIT MENU:  Hold START.

===============================================================================

* D-DOWN:        Town Portal.

[SEC7] SHOPS & SERVICES
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Logic: D-Pad directions follow the visual menu list order:
  1st (Up/North) | 2nd (Right/East) | 3rd (Down/South) | 4th (Left/West)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

* BLACKSMITH / TEMPLE / TRAINING (Y): Hold Y + Direction.
    - D-UP:      Buy / Heal / Train (1st).
    - D-RIGHT:   Armor / Donate (2nd).
    - D-DOWN:    Sell / Uncurse (3rd).

    * NESTED BUY MENU:  D-UP: Weapons | D-RIGHT: Armor | D-DOWN: Misc.
    * NESTED SELL MENU: D-UP: Sell | D-RIGHT: Repair | D-DOWN: Identify.

* TAVERN / GUILD (Z): Hold Z + Direction.
    - D-UP:      Drink / Info (1st).
    - D-RIGHT:   Food / Buy Spells (2nd).
    - D-DOWN:    Tip / Spell Info (3rd).
    - D-LEFT:    Rumor (4th).

* BANK (X):      Hold X + Direction.
    - D-UP/DN:   Gold / Gems.
    - D-LEFT:    Deposit (Hold X).
    - D-RIGHT:   Withdraw (Hold X).

===============================================================================
[SEC12] SCENARIO WALKTHROUGHS
===============================================================================

[A] TRADING GEAR:
    1. LT + Hold X: Opens Character 1's inventory bag.
    2. D-Pad: Highlight the item you want to move.
    3. Hold RT + Tap B: Instantly "Gives" the item to Character 5. 
    4. Pro-Tip: If you keep holding B after the tap, the script will 
       automatically switch the view into Character 5's bag so you can 
       immediately equip the item.

[B] HEALING & MAGIC:
    1. LT + Tap Z: Briefly switches focus to Character 3 (the Cleric).
    2. Tap C: Opens the current spell list for the Cleric.
    3. D-Pad/Joystick: Scroll and highlight "Heal" or "Power Cure."
    4. START: Casts the spell.
    5. LT + Tap X: Selects Character 1 as the target of the spell.

[C] BLACKSMITH BUY & SELL:
    1. Hold Y + D-UP: Opens the "Buy" menu at the blacksmith.
    2. Hold Y + D-RIGHT: Selects "Armor" from the nested shop list.
    3. START: Confirms the purchase of the highlighted item.
    4. To Exit: Tap SELECT (Esc) to back out of the nested menu.
    5. Hold Y + D-DOWN: Opens the "Sell" menu.
    6. Hold Y + D-UP: Selects "Sell" from the internal blacksmith list.
    7. Hold Y: Confirms the "Yes" prompt to complete the transaction.

[D] TRAVELING VIA MAGIC MIRROR:
    1. Walk into a Magic Mirror in any town.
    2. Click Left Joystick (L3) + [Y]: Automatically types "SEADOG" to 
       transport the party to Baywatch.
    3. If the password fails or you need a custom one: Click L3 to open 
       the Keyboard (OSK), type the name, and press START (Enter).
